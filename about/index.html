About the Author

Eleni Fkiaras


Eleni is a twenty-two years old, originally from San Jose.
She is one half Greek and one half Panamanian and she speaks Spanish and Greek.
She is a recent graduate from the University of San Francisco with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration- majoring in Marketing with a minor in Design.
She was a member of USF Division one Women's Track and Field Team. Eleni holds the USF record in the 400 meter hurdles. She has been running track for nine years.
Eleni enjoys exploring the city, trying new foods, reading, traveling, photography, and spending time with family.
