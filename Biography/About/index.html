Nicholas Felton


A Quantified Life

For the information designer Nicholas Felton, who used to work at Facebook sees the more data the better. He has counted all the places he has been, people been with, mile he has walk, books he has read, and so much more. He is an information designer who uses data to communicate and tell stories. Collecting all this data requires him to take a lot of notes. He uses customary apps and wears a Fitbit. For a number of years he has released personal Annual Reports . To compile the report for 2013 he gathered 94,834 data points. This included 44,041 text messages, 31,769 emails and 4,511 Facebook messages. He also tracked the 1,719 pieces of mail he sent and received and documented the 12,464 face-to-face conversations he had.
