Daytum.com

a website for collecting and communcating your daily data

Before all the calorie counting or sleep-tracking app, there was the original life tracking in Feltron's Annual Report. Since then, Nicholas elaborated his idea with Ryan Case, who is now at Facebook to create this website and app that allows users to generate their own data diaries. They release an iPhone version of Daytum, which let's you collect, categorize, and communicate data points for any random activity.
