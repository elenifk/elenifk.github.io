Reporter

new application for understanding the things you care about

a website for collecting and communcating your daily data

Most widely know for his work on Facebook's Timeline and the Annual Reports, Nicholas Felton has been working on something else. Partnering up with a friend, Drew Breunig they coded an iPhone app called the Reporter that would simplify the life recording process. Every 90 minutes, the app buzzes and automatically receives the coordinates of your location asking the questions of: Where are you? Who are you with? What are you wearing? How productive were you today (on a scale of 1- 50? Reporter is available on the App Store.
