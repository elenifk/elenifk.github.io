Personal Annual Reports


Nicholas Felton


Each year, graphic designer Nicholas Felton creates an annual report summarizing an entire year of his life with beautiful charts and graphs. He has published reports starting in 2005 until 2013. They are a compilation of information that he has personally collected and analyzed. Each year, he adds a new twist to the reports. Each report is well designed at storytelling all the information. This led Nicholas to start a Web Site called Daytum that helps people to track and visual things. The Annual Report personal received a lot of attention, so it elevated his career enormously. Felton uses the reports to push boundaries of visual data, to create innovative info graphics.
