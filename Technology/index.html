The Mobile Phone

Past

From the brick phone, to the clamshell, and to the side kicks...Cell Phones have evolved immensely since 1983, both in design and function. The first handhelp cell phone, the Motorola DynaTAC 8000X was invented on March 13, 1984. It sold for $3,995. Though huge by today's standards, it was considered the first mobile phone because it was small enough to carry. Since then, technology has advanced and companies figured out how to pack all the features their customers wanted into a smaller, portable, more affordable mode. Early cellphones primarily were just for talking. Not everyone carried cellphones as they were mainly for business or emergency. The earliest smart phones let users access email, and use phone as a fax machine, pager, and address book.

Present

In recent years, the purpose of the cell phone has shifted from a verbal communication tool to a multimedia tool, often adopting the name "mobile device" rather than being called a phone at all. We now use our cell phones more for surfing the web, checking email, snapping photos, and updating our social media status than actually placing calls. The cell phones of today are also replacing our other gadgets, such as cameras and video cameras. When cameras were first introduced on phones, the images were low quality and the feature was considered to just be an extra. However, today most people only take photos with their cell phone. Cellphone are closely related to the idea of pocket computer. We are constantly wirelessly connnect through our cellphones and has created a culture of instant information. Today, we feel naked if we do not have our cellphones on us. Smart phones exists as Apple or Andriod devices. Viral communication dominates today, as we constanlty are texting or checking our emails. Addditionally, the cellphone culture created new serivces that rely and are only possible with our cellphones today. Consider the service Lyft and Uber. Also, cellphones have a social impact on society today. The phrases EMOJIS , SELFIE, or #HASHTAG are possible with the advancement of cellphones.

future

The future brings some exciting possibilities for the cellphone. Cellphones will continue to improve. Already there are vast improvement with Apple Watch and Google Glasses. Google glasses remeble standard glasses with the lens replaced by a head-up display. Future cellphone will continue to get thinner, faster, and better. Future cellphone will not even be hand held devices. Hologram technology is projected to be implemented into cellphones.
